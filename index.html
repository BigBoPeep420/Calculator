Edit Custom Functions

Here you can enter your own custom expression and map it to one of the F buttons.

Your expression evaluates two variables (a and b) and returns a number. Use standard operators and do not include a return statement. Use by pushing the corresponding F button in place of an operator. There's a simple example below.

(a + 23) - b returns 28 if a=10 and b=5
